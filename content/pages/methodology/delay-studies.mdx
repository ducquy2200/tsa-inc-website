methodProfile:
  objective: Assess where vehicles are delayed, how long stoppages persist, and how queue dynamics change across conditions.
  tools:
    - Observation or video capture stations
    - Queue-length tracking forms
    - Time-interval logging templates
    - Reporting charts and trend summaries
  processSteps:
    - title: Delay objective framing
      description: Define target intersections or corridors and the specific delay questions the study must answer.
    - title: Stoppage and queue capture
      description: Record stopped time and queue lengths across selected intervals and operating periods.
    - title: Condition comparison
      description: Compare delay and queue patterns between peak and non-peak conditions.
    - title: Findings and recommendations
      description: Document critical delay points and propose practical interventions for traffic flow improvement.
  capturedData:
    - Vehicle stoppage duration by location
    - Queue lengths over time
    - Period-based congestion conditions
    - Location-specific delay pattern notes
  analysisOutputs:
    - Delay hotspot identification
    - Queue behavior trend summaries
    - Operational friction diagnosis
    - Improvement recommendation package
  standards:
    - Capture windows are aligned to known congestion conditions before fieldwork.
    - Queue measurements follow a consistent definition across all observation points.
    - Final reports include charts and interpretation notes for stakeholders.
  durationRules:
    - Duration is configured to include representative peak and non-peak intervals.
    - Multi-period collection can be used when congestion behavior changes by day or context.
